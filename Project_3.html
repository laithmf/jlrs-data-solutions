Welcome to Mario in the World of Bowser! Riding on the back of Flappy Bird, Mario must dodge towering pillars, evade Bowser's fireballs, and avoid falling into the fiery depths of the Dungan lava. Your quick reflexes and strategic thinking will be put to the test as you guide Mario through each challenging level. The fate of the Mushroom Kingdom depends on it!
